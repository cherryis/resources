Types of Sensors and Uses yaantra.com(Article)
  • Accelerometer(Phone's orientation): Tracking footsteps, Screen view change between portrait to landscape
  • Ambient Temperature: Detecting humidity, temperature, and pressure
  • Magnetometer (Digital Compass): Metal detector
  • Gyroscope(advance version of Accelerometer): VR apps, maps,360-degree video, game, Working with X,Y, Z-axis.
  • Heart Rate: fitness
  • Ambient Light: Auto-adjusts the brightness level include under direct sunlight
  • Proximity : Lie at top of the phone's front side - turning off the display during phone call
  • Pressure (Barometer) : Detecting the present altitude from the sea level
  • Biometric Scanners(fingerprint scanner, iris scanner, full-face detection sensors) : Security for authorized people can unlock the phone through their eye, fingerprints, face
  • Pedometer: More accurate than Accelerometer
